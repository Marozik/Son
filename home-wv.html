Our story

Experience & Sons Steakeasy: a gastronomic haven in West Village, NYC. Discover a sophisticated bar, hidden speakeasy, and gourmet dining room, merging tradition, creativity, and culinary excellence. Indulge in handcrafted cocktails, Prohibition-era intrigue, and sublime feasts where prime meats meet local ingredients. Join us at & Sons Steakeasy and become part of our tale of tradition, innovation, and culinary exploration.

RESTAURANT

Dive into & Sons, the culinary gem where fine dining embraces steakhouse traditions. Relish our chefs' masterpieces, blending prime cuts and local ingredients into flavorful dishes. At & Sons, every meal becomes a celebration.
